Mom's Spanish Rice|An excellent Spanish rice recipe that goes well with any traditional Hispanic dish. By Victor Espinoza.|4|10|25|2 tablespoons oil@1 1/2 cups uncooked white rice@2 1/2 cups chicken broth@2 tablespoons salt@1 1/2 cups chopped tomatoes@1/2 medium onion, diced@1/2 clove garlic, minced|Heat oil in a large, heavy skillet over medium heat.@Stir in onion and garlic. Cook until tender, about 5 minutes.@Mix rice into skillet, stirring often.@Mix tomatoes and chicken broth and blend together until the tomatoes are liquefied@When rice begins to brown, stir in blended ingredients.@Reduce heat, cover skillet with lid, and let it simmer until the liquid has been absorbed into the rice, about 20 minutes.
